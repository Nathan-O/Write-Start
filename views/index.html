Editing:

"Don't be a d@%k."

Wil Wheaton Twitter

This project is meant for writers of all skill levels, profession levels, and styles/types. Please keep all comments and edit on a constructive and for the benefit of the story only. If what you have to say is against the writer or generally unhelpful...maybe skip it.

 

Being edited:

No one enjoys having their babies torn apart, their precious words and descriptions chopped and gutted with the ominous red ink; but a good story needs good editing.

 

“Kill your darlings, kill your darlings, even when it breaks your egocentric little scribbler’s heart, kill your darlings.”

Stephen King On Writing: A Memoir of the Craft

And who better to help you edit than others who share your passion. The aim of the game is to keep a thick skin and find what the editor meant with a particular cross out or suggested change. You can't be 100% objective about your own words, so take all edits into consideration, and keep in mind that the work is yours - any edits you don't agree with can simply be ignored at your leisure.
